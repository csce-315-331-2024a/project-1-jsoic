About Me

My name is Julian Stoicoviciu, I am a passionate computing major from Austin, Texas with a strong interest in cybersecurity and software development. My journey into the world of technology began when I was in high school. I took a robotics course because I heard it was an easy ‘A’. In the class, I was introduced to coding and the world of technology. I learned to write code for my team’s robot to compete in the FIRST Tech competition. The single class I thought I’d take as an easy A soon became my passion. Since then, I have been dedicated to mastering the skills necessary to make a meaningful impact in the tech industry. Over the years, I have developed my abilities in various programming languages, including Python, Java, and C++, and have become proficient in web development using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Beyond academics, I have always been curious about the inner workings of different operating systems, and the realm of cybersecurity.
